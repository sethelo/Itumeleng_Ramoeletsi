Frequently Asked Questions

What types of electronic waste do you recycle?

We recycle a wide range of electronic waste, including RAM, hard drives, motherboards, and other computer components.

How do you ensure data security during recycling?

We use advanced data destruction techniques to ensure all data is permanently erased before recycling.

Can I recover data from a damaged hard drive?

Yes, our data recovery tools can help retrieve data from damaged or corrupted hard drives.
